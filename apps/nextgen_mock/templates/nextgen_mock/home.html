Help and Tips
  • Use the icon to copy the case number to your clipboard
  • Use the icon to open the case on Secure Access
  • Use the icon to search for the case number on Secure Access
  • Use the icon to view the Public Access Case Info in a pop-up
  • Use the icon to view scanned docket images
  • Hover over the abbreviations to view the full text
  • When viewing search results, at times a "Search" field is available to filter the records displayed (for example, filter by case number, name, jurisdiction, etc.)
  • When filtering results, to view only cases without an event result, type the word "pending" or a back tick ` into the search field. The backtick key is below the "Esc" key and to the left of the "1" key.
  • When filtering results, Multiple search terms can be used at the same time. For example, open an eCourt Docket for Courtroom 1A Criminal-Traffic and try searching for "OHP speed pending".
  • Certain case information may not be available online due to confidentiality, court orders, or other relevant considerations.
  • The Secure Access platform is a means to provide limited information about cases, but it may not always reflect the complete and up-to-date status of a case. For the most accurate record, individuals are directed to consult the relevant Clerk's Office division, Clerk's Case Management System, or physical case file.
  • For performance reasons, data may be cached to decrease page load times.
